About Us


How We Got Our Start...

Supply Chain Services is a family and friends company. We opened the doors in 2002 and have truly been blessed by the people that work for us and with us. We are an asset-based company that truly thrives on the challenge of new projects and difficult needs. We offer our customers a wide variety of fleet options and specialty trailers but mainly are a specialized heavy haul logistics company. Fleet options include: power only, flatbed, flatbed extendable, step deck, step deck with loading ramp, Landoll, RGN, Stretch RGN, 8 Axle Steerable Dolly, 10 Axle Jeep & Steerable Dolly, 11 Axle Jeep & Steerable Dolly, and so many other options. Our drivers are Hazmat, TWIC, and Tanker endorsed and we even have some that run teams for us. We really do have a fabulous family that surrounds us and we are lucky to call this place home. We hope you will call it home as well.
